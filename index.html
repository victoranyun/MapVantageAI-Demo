Map the impact of automation in your community
















Our question:


What are the key indicators of differences in automation vulnerability between neighborhoods in a given city?





MapVantage uses the power of data and machine learning to inform city stakeholders on those most vulnerable to the impact and risks of automation.





Diversity is Canada’s strength

Not every neighborhood is the same. Research shows that labour market disruption does not affect everyone equally and this can have profound destabilizing effects. As technology advances at an increasing rate, those most vulnerable to the impacts include a wide range of demographics, including minority populations, immigrants, low income households, young workers, and those with disabilities.

01.

Data:

Integrates current and projected socio-economic data on vulnerable groups.

02.

Machine Learning:

Incorporates the intelligence of machine learning to find vulnerable clusters.

03.

Risk Mitigation:

Provides a clearer picture of risk through a more detailed lens.

04.

Stakeholder Collaboration:

Enables stakeholders to collaborate on community-based initiatives.

05.

Policy Tool:

Directs policy mechanisms in a more tailored way.

06.

Delivers Understanding:

Gives municipal and community leaders a clearer understanding of constituents across a wide-range of vulnerable groups.
